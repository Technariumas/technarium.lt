Join Technarium!

Technarium is an independent hackerspace/workshop based in Vilnius, Lithuania. We are a non-commercial community initiative sustained by membership fees we use to cover the rent and other expenses.

If you would like to become a member, contact us: we'll show you around on Monday nights (18:00-21:00). In order to accomodate all different needs and abilities, we offer the following membership levels starting on January 2020:

0. Visitor/starving hacker membership -- 16€/month.
A special case: for those who just hang out here occasionally or who are away at the moment but do not wish to cancel their membership. Also for those who like the idea of an open community working space and would like to support us. Visitor members are welcome when other members are at the space. They can use the common area, kitchen and wireless Internet access.
1. Standard membership -- 47€/month.
Members can get a key to the space, some storage place for their stuff, use the tools and equipment in the workshops (wood, metal, electronics, 3D printing, etc.). Use of some equipment, such as welders, machines, ceramics kilns, etc. is only allowed after an initial period of membership and training. All machines and power tools require initial training.
2. Supporter membership -- 55+/mėn.
For those who can afford larger standard membership fees.
3. Own Corner -- TBD individually.
A personal working space in Technarium for you and your friends. Some working space you don't have to tidy up before leaving + storage space. Subject to availability of space.
4. Freelancer membership -- 170€/month, to be determined depending on the work type and needs.
For those who would be working at Technarium and use the tools and equipment on a daily basis.
4. A one-off large project or commercial membership -- TBD on a case-by-case base.
For those who would like to work on their large one-off project at Technarium, commercial entities, funded institutions.

We are a community, not landlords. The community and people with their ideas make this place come alive and are more important than finance. If you cannot afford full membership but would like to make something useful or interesting for everybody, let's talk. We shall never close the door to good initiatives because of money.
